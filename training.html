OUR PROGRAMS

From high-intensity cardio to technical strength training, we have a program designed for your evolution.

01. Strength Training

Build a foundation of power. Our strength training programs focus on compound movements, progressive overload, and proper form to help you pack on lean muscle and increase bone density.

  • Powerlifting Foundations
  • Hypertrophy Focused Workouts
  • Olympic Weightlifting

Head Coaches: Utsho & Alex

Our strength curriculum is built on the Big Three: Squat, Bench, and Deadlift. We provide customized lifting blocks that track your 1RM progression and volume load over 12-week cycles.

02. HIIT & Cardio

Torch calories and improve your cardiovascular health. Our HIIT sessions are designed to push your heart rate to the limit, ensuring maximum fat burn in minimum time.

  • Metabolic Conditioning
  • Tabata Style Intervals
  • Endurance Drills

Head Coach: Sarah Jenkins

Our HIIT zone is equipped with Assault Bikes, Concept2 Rowers, and SkiErgs. Classes use MyZone heart rate tracking to ensure you stay in the optimal fat-burning zones.

03. Yoga & Mobility

Recovery is just as important as the workout. Our mobility and yoga classes help reduce injury risk, improve flexibility, and provide a mental reset after intense training sessions.

  • Vinyasa Flow
  • Dynamic Stretching
  • Mindful Meditation

Head Coach: Mike Ross

Beyond flexibility, our mobility sessions focus on joint longevity and fascial release. We incorporate foam rolling, PNF stretching, and Vinyasa flows.
